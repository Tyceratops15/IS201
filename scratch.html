The Characters

  1. Richard
    • The unreliable narrator of the book
    • A californian who joins the group late
    • Is entranced bv the group and their personalities
  2. Henry
    • Wealthy and learned in many languages
    • Is the mastermind behind most of the groups schemes
    • Is well-liked and respected amongst the group
  3. Bunny
    • The victim of the groups schemes
    • Loud and obnoxious
    • Full of crude, ill-timed jokes
  4. Charles
    • The twin brother of Camilla
    • Dressed in tennis clothes often, more relaxed then the others
    • A unrealized alcoholic
  5. Camilla
    • The twin sister of Charles
    • Short, blonde hair that entrances Richard and the other guys
    • The only girl in the group
  6. Francis
    • The unspoken outsider of the group
    • Has a city in the country that the group resides at often
    • Dresses the nicest and is more formal
